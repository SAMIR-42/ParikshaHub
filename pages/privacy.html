Privacy Policy

Your data security is our top priority at ParikshaHub.

Last Updated: March 2026

🔐 Information We Collect

We collect only essential information such as teacher account details, student basic identity (Name, Class, Roll Number), and exam responses required to conduct secure and fair examinations.

🛡️ How We Protect Your Data

ParikshaHub uses secure server infrastructure, encrypted connections, protected APIs, and strict access controls to prevent unauthorized access, data leaks, or misuse.

👁️ Exam Monitoring & Anti-Cheat Systems

To maintain exam integrity, we use automated monitoring systems such as tab-switch detection, fullscreen enforcement, activity tracking, and secure session validation. These systems ensure a fair environment for all students.

🚫 No Data Selling Policy

We strictly do NOT sell, rent, or share user data with advertisers, marketing agencies, or third parties under any circumstances.

☁️ Secure Cloud Storage

All exam data, results, and user information are stored on protected cloud servers with multi-layer security and regular safety audits.

📞 Support & Grievances

If you have privacy concerns, you may contact our support team. We respond to all legitimate requests within 48 working hours.
