Support & Help

Quick answers for teachers. Still stuck? Use the refund page or email us — we reply as soon as we can.

Payment & test not showing

Money deducted but test not in “My Tests”? Wait 1–2 minutes (bank + webhook), then open the dashboard again. If it still doesn’t appear, read our refund policy and follow the steps there.

Refund policy & steps

Try this first

  • 1
    Refresh the dashboard (or log out and log in once) after returning from payment.
  • 2
    Before paying, make sure subject, class, and every question (options, marks, time, correct answer) is filled — then tap Create Test again.
  • 3
    If the payment page won’t load or errors out, try another browser or network (Wi‑Fi / mobile data).
  • 4
    Use desktop for creating tests — mobile popups can sometimes block fields or file uploads.

Other questions

Write to us at parikshahub.support@gmail.com — mention your registered email and what you were doing (e.g. “payment done, test missing”).
