How It Works

Simple steps to create and conduct your test

Create Test in 1 Flow

  • 1 Click Create Test
  • 2 New user? Signup • Existing? Login
  • 3 Fill test details + add questions
  • 4 After all set, Click Create Test
  • 5 Complete a small amount secure payment for creating Test
  • 6 After payment successfull, Test appears on your dashboard
  • 7 Copy link or share via QR

Student Exam Flow

  • 1 Open exam via Link / QR
  • 2 Read instructions & enter Name, Roll, Class
  • 3 Start exam → get one question at a time
  • 4 Each question has fixed timer set by teacher
  • 5 Time ends → next question auto appears
  • 6 Click image to zoom & view clearly
  • 7 After exam → instant score shown
  • 8 Teacher gets result with Name + Marks
  • 9 Download full student result in 1 click

Create Test in 1 Flow

  • 1 Questions are read-only (no copy allowed)
  • 2 Exam runs in fullscreen mode
  • 3 1st tab switch → 1 min warning timer
  • 4 2nd switch → 3 min strict penalty
  • 5 3rd switch → 7 min lock timer
  • 6 4th switch → Exam auto submit
  • 7 Multiple refresh → auto submit
  • 8 Same roll no → re-attempt blocked
  • 9 Teacher can allow re-test by deleting result
  • 10 All results update live in dashboard
