Refund Policy

Transparent & Fair Refund Rules for Teachers

Eligibility for Refund

  • Payment deducted but test not created
  • Duplicate payment for same test
  • Technical server failure during payment
  • Refund request within 48 hours of transaction

Refund Not Applicable

  • Test already created successfully
  • Teacher entered incorrect details
  • Network issues from user's side
  • Change of mind after purchase

Refund Verification Process

  1. Teacher submits payment proof
  2. Transaction ID is verified
  3. System logs are checked
  4. Approval decision within 3 working days

Refund Method

Approved refunds are credited back to the original payment method. Processing time may take 5–7 business days depending on bank.

We ensure fair investigation before every refund to prevent misuse.
